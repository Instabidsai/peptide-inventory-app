## Mechanism of Action

SS31 operates through a unique mechanism that targets the inner mitochondrial membrane. It possesses an electrical charge that allows it to bind effectively to this membrane, which is crucial for energy production in cells. 

1. **Reactive Oxygen Species (ROS) Scavenging**: SS31 scavenges ROS at the source, neutralizing them as they are generated during the electron transport chain (ETC) processes. This prevents oxidative damage to mitochondrial structures.

2. **Stabilization of Cardiolipin**: SS31 stabilizes cardiolipin, a phospholipid essential for maintaining the structural integrity of the mitochondrial membrane. By protecting cardiolipin from oxidative stress, SS31 helps maintain the functionality of the ETC, thereby enhancing ATP production.
